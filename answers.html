ANSWERS

Now you know if you are a real soundtracker!!!

1. From which saga is this song?
✘ The Lord of the Rings
✘ The Chronicles of Narnia
✔ Harry Potter
✘ Percy Jackson

★ Fun fact ★

During all the eight movies, there are different themes made by many composers created for particular locations (Diagon Alley), characters (Voldemort) and items (Harry’s family portrait). But, throughout the series, just a few songs lasted for all the films.
2. Of which film was the first movie soundtrack album ever commercially released?
✘ King Kong (1933)
✔ Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs (1937)
✘ Fantasia (1940)
✘ The Jazz Singer (1927)

★ Fun fact ★

Two songs were made for the animated movie Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, “You're Never Too Old to Be Young” and “Music in Your Soup”, but they were not included.
3. From which tv series is this song?
✔ Stranger Things
✘ Shadowhunters
✘ Twin Peaks
✘ I Am Not Okay with This

★ Fun fact ★

We must thank Nora Felder for the perfectly chosen songs in the entire series. As the longtime music supervisor of Stranger Things, she got four Emmy nominations.
4. What is the best-selling movie soundtrack of all time?
✔ The Bodyguard soundtrack
✘ The Dirty Dancing soundtrack
✘ The Titanic soundtrack
✘ The Grease soundtrack

★ Fun fact ★

The success of the six pop songs performed by Whitney Houston in the movie, including “I Will Always Love You”, “I Have Nothing” and “I'm Every Woman”, made her the first female act to have three songs in the Top 20 simultaneously.
5. From which Disney movie is this song?
✘ The Little Mermaid
✘ Cinderella
✘ Finding Nemo
✔ Beauty and the Beast

★ Fun fact ★

The main producer of the Beauty and the Beast soundtrack, Howard Ashman, although he had recently been diagnosed with AIDS, insisted on working on the song’s lyrics, even on his deathbed.
6. What is the biggest selling Disney song of all time?
✘ Can You Feel the Love Tonight, The Lion King
✘ Let It Go, Frozen
✔ We Don’t Talk About Bruno, Encanto
✘ A Whole New World, Aladdin

★ Fun fact ★

Even if the lyrics of "We Don't Talk About Bruno" suggest that Bruno is a villain, the song shows different characters' perspectives of him, unlike all the other Disney villain songs.
7. From which video game is this song?
✘ The Sims
✔ Animal Crossing
✘ Little Dragon Cafe
✘ Happy Street

★ Fun fact ★

Animal Crossing: New Horizons game has actually seven CDs worth of theme music.
8. Who composed the musics for Jaws, Saving Private Ryan and Jurassic Park, and is the most famous soundtrack composer?
✔ John Williams
✘ Hans Zimmer
✘ Ennio Morricone
✘ John Barry

★ Fun fact ★

John William has even composed music for four Olympic Games: the 1984 Olympics in Los Angeles, the 1988 Olympics in Seoul, the 1996 Olympics in Atlanta and the 2002 Olympics in Salt Lake City.
9. From which video game is this song?
✘ Oblivion
✘ The Witcher
✔ The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim
✘ Dark Souls

★ Fun fact ★

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim's original soundtrack track list can be bought on Amazon and has got more than fifty different songs, which can last more than forty minutes.
10. (I've Had) The Time of My Life, Hungry Eyes and She's Like the Wind were the theme songs for which hit-film?
✘ Pretty Woman
✘ Flashdance
✘ Footloose
✔ Dirty Dancing

★ Fun fact ★

The More Dirty Dancing album was released in 1988, a year after the release of the film. This album was not the last one, because in 2003 the RCA made a third one, which contains every song from Dirty Dancing in order of appearance.
